# Privacy Policy

**Investment Growth Strategist**  
*Last Updated: April 2025*

## Overview

Investment Growth Strategist is a tool that runs primarily in your browser within ChatGPT. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

- **Email Address**: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter via the in-widget subscribe form. Your email is sent to Buttondown (our email service provider) to manage subscriptions.
- **Coarse Location**: When the tool is invoked via ChatGPT, we may receive approximate location information (city, region, country, timezone) provided by the ChatGPT client. This is used solely for anonymous analytics (e.g., understanding regional usage patterns).
- **Device & Browser Category**: We classify your device into broad categories (iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, Linux) from the user-agent hint provided by ChatGPT. No raw user-agent strings are stored long-term.
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage data including tool invocations, response times, and investment parameters you provide (e.g., balance, contribution amounts). These are used to monitor service health and improve the calculator.
- **User Feedback**: If you voluntarily submit feedback via the in-widget feedback form, the text you enter is logged to help us improve the product.
- **Error Logs**: Anonymous error information to improve reliability.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Financial account credentials or banking information
- Social Security Numbers or government IDs
- Credit card or payment information
- Protected health information (PHI)
- Precise GPS coordinates or street addresses
- Raw chat transcripts or full conversation history

## Data Processing

All investment calculations are performed:
- **Client-side**: In your browser within the ChatGPT sandbox
- **Locally**: Your investment data is processed locally and not stored on our servers beyond what is described above

## Third-Party Services

- **Buttondown** (https://buttondown.email): Used to manage email subscriptions if you opt in. Buttondown's own privacy policy governs how they handle your email address.

## Data Storage

- **Browser LocalStorage**: Your calculator inputs may be cached in your browser's localStorage for convenience (72-hour expiration). This data never leaves your device.
- **Server Logs**: Analytics are logged for up to 30 days for service improvement, then automatically deleted.

## Data Retention

- **LocalStorage**: Automatically expires after 72 hours
- **Server Logs**: Retained for up to 30 days, then deleted
- **Email Subscriptions**: Retained until you unsubscribe

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove cached data
- Use the calculator without providing any personal information
- Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email
- Request deletion of your data by contacting us

## Security

- All communications use HTTPS encryption (HTTP is for local development only)
- The widget runs in a sandboxed iframe with strict Content Security Policy
- No sensitive financial credentials are transmitted or stored

## Children's Privacy

This service is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be noted in the app documentation.

## Contact

For privacy questions, support, or data deletion requests:
- **Email**: support@layer3labs.io
- **Deletion requests**: Include the UTC timestamp of your ChatGPT session; we will delete associated logs within 7 business days.
